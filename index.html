About InAmigos

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit NGO started on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla.

It is based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh and it is a Section 8 registered organisation.

The foundation works in many states of India. They help people with food, education, women empowerment, animal care and environment protection.

InAmigos is 80G and 12A certified. This means people who donate can also get tax benefit.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To serve the community with kindness and to help poor and needy people through different social projects.

Vision

To give every underprivileged person a chance to live with dignity and grow better in life.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project Seva

Under this project, food and clothes are given to poor and homeless people. Regular food drives are done to help hungry families.

Project Bachpanshala

This project gives free education and study material to children who cannot afford school due to poverty.

Project Udaan

Project Udaan helps women learn new skills so they can earn money and become independent.

Project Prakriti

This project is about planting trees and taking care of nature so the environment stays clean and green.

Social Impact

Because of these projects, many families get food regularly and do not sleep hungry.

Many poor children are now going to school and learning new things.

Women in villages are learning skills and starting to earn their own money.

Thousands of trees are planted which is helping the environment.

The NGO has also trained more than 30,000 interns under Project Vikas, which helps students gain experience.
